Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential tech conferences globally, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, share knowledge, and empower the next generation of tech leaders. We are committed to fostering an inclusive, collaborative, and forward-thinking tech community.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Morgan

A pioneer in AI ethics, Dr. Morgan has shaped global policy around responsible AI deployment. Her keynote at TechCon 2022 inspired over 5,000 attendees to adopt ethical tech practices.

Jamal Kimani

As CTO of FutureNet, Jamal led innovations in 5G networks and decentralized cloud computing. His talks are known for blending deep tech with humor and real-world case studies.
